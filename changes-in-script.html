Changes made in the Latin Script of Sindhi so far

03-Feb-2020

Removed d̈ and d̤ as ڌ and ڍ as they could have been written as 'dh' and 'ḍh'.

20-Feb-2020

Replaced ş, t̆, t̑ with sh, ẗh and th respectively. Ú was removed from the alphabet.

26-Mar-2020

Replaced ň and ř with ń and ŕ for better readability.
